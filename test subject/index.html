HOW DRUGS KILL US

The Dangers of Drug Abuse

Drug abuse can lead to devastating consequences, including overdose, addiction, and even death. It's essential to understand the risks and make informed decisions about substance use.

The Effects of Drug Abuse

  • Physical Health Problems: Organ damage, respiratory issues, and increased risk of overdose.
  • Mental Health Issues: Depression, anxiety, and psychosis.
  • Social and Relationship Problems: Strained relationships, loss of employment, and social isolation.

Get Help

If you or someone you know is struggling with drug abuse, there are resources available:

  • SAMHSA National Helpline
  • National Institute on Drug Abuse

Warning Signs

Be aware of the warning signs of drug abuse, including:

  • Changes in behavior or mood
  • Increased secrecy or isolation
  • Physical signs of substance use, such as needle marks or changes in appetite
